Motivation

To assist Bridge to Calculus in its mission to help disadvantaged young adults in Boston gain the competitive edge they need to succeed in calculus classes and beyond.

There are many students in Boston who are striving for help with their math homeworks, but not have access to sufficient resources and assist. Our group's mission is to support Bridge To Calculus by creating materials that prove the effectiveness of Bridge To Calculus. We focused on proving how students who participated in Bridge To Calculus improved over time, which will be used as a tool to appeal to potential donors.
