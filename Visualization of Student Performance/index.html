What is this Project

The main objective of this project was to help high school students prepare for future AP and advanced calculus courses in order to reduce the achievement gap between low and high-income students and help put them on the path to college. As such, the program is catered to students who might otherwise be unable to access tutoring due to schedule constraints or a lack of funds. The graphs developed and the analysis carried out during this investigation were not only useful in order to find trends, draw conclusions, and give advice to the teachers, but they also allowed us to showcase the performance of the students in this school.
